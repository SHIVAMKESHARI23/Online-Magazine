Uttarkashi Tunnel Rescue Operation Live Updates: All Workers Rescued, PM Calls It "Matter Of Great Satisfaction"



Uttarkashi Tunnel Rescue Operation Live Updates: All the forty-one workers who were trapped inside Uttarakhand's Silkyari tunnel since November 12 were evacuated safely today. The rescued workers were draped in orange marigold flower garlands and were greeted by Uttarakhand Chief Minister Pushkar Singh Dhami and Union Minister VK Singh. The trapped workers were pulled out through 57 metres of steel pipe on stretchers fitted with wheels. Prime Minister Narendra Modi led the nation in praising the courage and patience of the trapped workers and their families and the bravery and determination of the rescue personnel.
